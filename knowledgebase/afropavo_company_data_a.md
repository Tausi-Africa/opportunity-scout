# Consulting Team

### 4.1 Team Lead : Rwebugisa Mutahaba

With over two decades of experience, he has expertise in business strategy development, analytics, operations, sales, distribution, and digital marketing strategies. Mutahaba has held significant roles in prominent organizations, including CARE International in Tanzania, where he served as a Senior Project Manager for Chomoka(Digital Village Savings and Loan Associations Management System). He also worked as the Country Manager for JUMO, overseeing substantial monthly disbursements and spearheading product launches. Mutahaba holds a Bachelor's degree in Economic History from the London School of Economics and Political Science and a Postgraduate degree in Business/Managerial Economics from Cardiff University.

# 5.0 Relevant Previous Work 

Afropavo Analytics demonstrated expertise in assisting financial service providers grow and scale,  by analyzing financial services across ten African countries: Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria, Senegal, Cameroon, Côte d’Ivoire, and South Africa. Mapping financial service providers, evaluating user perceptions through a robust platform, and developing actionable strategies to close financial inclusion gaps. Using human-centered design, stakeholder engagement, and scenario-based approaches, aligning with FSDT’s Market Systems Development methodology, addressing financial challenges for underserved populations, particularly youth and women. In which they have served multiple financial service providers through FSDT’s Finclinic program : Britam Tanzania , Zanzibar Social Security Fund , RLabs Iringa and CRDB Bank Foundation.

From 2021 to 2024, Afropavo Analytics supported Wakandi Tanzania in developing and localizing its Credit Association Management System (CAMS) for MFIs and SACCOS. Conducting business analysis, facilitating stakeholder workshops, and providing user-centric recommendations to enhance scalability. Afropavo built a local tech team for ongoing support and developed a market entry strategy for Kenya, ensuring CAMS compliance with local regulations. Our work improved financial management for underserved communities, strengthened operational sustainability, and enabled Wakandi’s regional growth in East Africa. 

Afropavo Analytics partnered with FINCA Microfinance Bank to drive financial inclusion through a streamlined, paperless biometric onboarding tool. Leveraging advanced biometric technologies like touchless fingerprint and facial recognition, the solution simplified customer onboarding while ensuring security and efficiency. Afropavo conducted needs analysis, iterative development, and integration with FINCA’s core banking system, along with comprehensive training for staff. This innovation reduced onboarding barriers, empowering underserved communities to access banking services and advancing FINCA’s mission of poverty alleviation through financial inclusion.
